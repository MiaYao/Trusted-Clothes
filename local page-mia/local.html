The clothing industry is the second highest polluting industry globally only behind Fossil Fuels.
Over 75 million people are employed worldwide in the garment industry, many of which are women and underage girls who are not earning a living wage.
Our skins is the largest organ in our body and it absorbs the chemicals it comes into contact with.

Trusted Clothes is sparking a global movement in the clothing industry. We bring people and communities together and deliver messages to change the world. Our mission is to change people's view on the garment industry by sharing stories online, educating the public, and taking research-based action. Based in Kitchener-Waterloo region, we are one of the fastest growing organizations attracting hundreds of volunteers all over the world. We empower, inspire, and grow with our volunteers.
